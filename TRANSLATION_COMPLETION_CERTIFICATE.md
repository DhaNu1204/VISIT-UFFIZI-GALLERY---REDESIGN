### Translation Style Guides

**Italian:**
- Mix of tu/Lei based on context
- Emphasis on cultural pride
- Keywords: biglietti, salta la fila, orari

**German:**
- Formal Sie throughout
- Detailed, practical information
- Keywords: Ohne Anstehen, Öffnungszeiten, Führung

**French:**
- Formal vous throughout
- Sophisticated, cultural depth
- Keywords: coupe-file, horaires, visite guidée

**Spanish:**
- Warm, personal tú form
- Emotional, experiential
- Keywords: entradas, sin colas, horario
